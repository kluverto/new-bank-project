About Evergreen Financial Bank

Building Financial Futures That Last
At Evergreen Financial, we believe money should be a source of stability, not stress.
Since our founding, our mission has been simple: to help individuals, families,
and businesses grow and protect their wealth with confidence, care, and clarity.
Like the evergreen tree, our commitment is steadfast year-round
no matter the season of your life or the market.

Our Promise
We’re here to make banking human again.
That means transparent products, personalized guidance, and a relationship built on trust.
Whether you’re opening your first savings account, buying a home, expanding your business,
or planning for retirement, we stand beside you with tools and support for every stage.

What We Offer:

  • Personal and Business Banking Solutions
  • Everyday Banking — Checking, savings, and payment solutions designed for simplicity and security.
  • Lending Solutions — Flexible mortgages, personal loans, and business financing tailored to your goals.
  • Wealth Management — Strategic investment and planning services to grow your future with confidence.
  • Digital Access — Secure online and mobile banking to keep you connected anytime, anywhere.

Our Values:

  • Integrity — We do what’s right, not what’s easy.
  • Community — We invest in the communities we serve.
  • Innovation — We embrace change to better serve you.
  • Excellence — We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Sustainability — We commit to responsible banking practices that support long-term well-being.

Rooted in Community
Evergreen Financial isn’t just a bank — we’re a neighbor.
We support local initiatives, champion financial literacy,
and give back to the places we call home.
Because your success is our success.
